Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wanted to learn about disc golf? Well, you are in the right place. We strive to aid you in getting started with just a disc and a large field. Here you will find all the important information to help you learn the basics of disc golf. This includes how to play disc golf, how to properly throw the disc (grips, technique), how to keep track of score, and most importantly how to enjoy your time in the outdoors. So, buckle up and prepare to not get tree-nied (pun).
