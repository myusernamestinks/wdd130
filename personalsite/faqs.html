FAQ's

Frequently asked questions

The aim of this page is to answer some of your most basic questions. If you can't find what you are looking for here. Please consider visiting pdga.com for more help.

  • How many disc should I have when playing Disc Golf? Typically, three disc are necessary. A driver, mid-range, and a putter disc should get you through a full game of disc golf.
  • Where can I find disc golf courses in my city? You can visit Disc Golf United Course Locator, UDisc, PDGA Course Directory.
  • Where Can I find more information about disc golf? The best place to find information is directly on the PDGA website.
